About Me

I'm a Backend Developer passionate about leveraging technology to fight climate change by making agriculture smarter and more sustainable. With a background in Environmental Chemistry, I'm building FarmConnect - a platform that assists farmers with data-driven decisions to reduce greenhouse gas emissions while improving harvests.

My journey into tech was inspired by my observations during my chemistry studies about how agriculture contributes significantly to greenhouse gas emissions despite low yields. I'm currently honing my backend development skills at ALX, focusing on building robust, scalable systems that can power applications like FarmConnect to make a real-world impact.
